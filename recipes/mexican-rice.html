Mexican Rice

This is an excelled authentic Mexican Rice recipe (not to be confused with Spanish rice) that I make as a side dish with all of my Mexican dishes. The key is cooking the rice properly and using good quality chicken broth

  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat and add rice.
  2. Cook, stirring constantly, until puffed and golden.
  3. While rice is cooking, sprinkly with salt and cumin.
  4. Stir in onions and cook until tender.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce and chicken broth; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat to low, cover and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  7. Fluff with a fork. Serve.
