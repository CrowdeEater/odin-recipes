Santa Cruz Red Chili Sauce

  • 1 Tbs. shortening or oil
  • 1 1/2 Tbs. flour
  • 1 cup Santa Cruz Chili Powder
  • 2 to 3 cups water or broth
  • 1/2 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. garlic powder
  • 1 cube beef bullion
  1. Brown shortening and flour over medium heat.
  2. Add chili powder, water, salt, garlic and bullion and, stirring continulously, bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes or until sauce reaches desired consistency.
  4. Serve over enchilades, chimichangas, or any recipe calling for red chili sauce.
